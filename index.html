BJMPRO3POS

BJMPRO3POS is a capstone project titled "Biometric Enabled Cashless Jail Comimissary Point of Sales and Inventory System"

This project is a Point of Sale System made for jail commissaries of the Bureau of Jail Management and Penology - Region 3.

This consists of various modules including inventory management, sales tracking, commissary statistics, and user biometric authentication.
